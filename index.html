About Me

My name is Kabelo Selomo, known by my stage name Arie SA. I’m a 24-year-old electronic music producer from Lesotho, creating soulful and deep house sounds that reflect my life’s emotions and energy.

I’m in a loving relationship with my girlfriend, Pontsho Motsoasele, who is a huge inspiration in my journey. I’m also a proud brother to Karabo Selomo and Rethabile Selomo, who’ve been with me every step of the way.
